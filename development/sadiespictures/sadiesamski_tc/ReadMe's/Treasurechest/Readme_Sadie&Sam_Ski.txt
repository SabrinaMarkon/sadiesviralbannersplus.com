Ski Outfits for Sadie and Sam. 
Ski Jackets, hat, skis, ski poles, boots, goggles and pants for Sadie. Material pose to add second skin pants to Sam. Poses to inj "No Ears" morphs to Sadie and Sam (will only work with Poser 6 onwards). Two sets of materials for the clothes and props.

Matching mats for gloves from Sk8 Protectors for Sam and Modern Girl gloves for Sadie.

For use in Poser 7 and possibly Poser 6. Not tested in DAZ Studio.

--------------------------------------

You will need:-  "Sadie" by 3D Universe
		 "Sam"   by 3D Universe

                 "Sk8 Protectors" by Elleque
		 "Modern Girl" by Elleque  

--------------------------------------

Installation Instructions:

Install using an unzip program directly into your Poser 7 library.
